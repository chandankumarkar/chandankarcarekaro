About CareKaro

Our Mission

CareKaro aims to revolutionize the healthcare supply chain by creating a direct connection between pharmacies and pharmaceutical manufacturers, eliminating middlemen and reducing costs.

We believe in making healthcare more accessible and affordable for everyone by optimizing the distribution network and ensuring timely delivery of essential medicines.

Our Vision

To create a seamless ecosystem where pharmacies can directly source medicines from manufacturers, ensuring authenticity, reducing costs, and improving availability of critical medications.

We envision a future where no patient has to suffer due to unavailability or high costs of essential medicines.

Key Features

Direct Ordering

Place orders directly with manufacturers without any intermediaries, ensuring better prices and authentic products.

Real-time Tracking

Track your orders in real-time from manufacturing to delivery, ensuring complete transparency.

Competitive Pricing

Get the best prices directly from manufacturers with bulk ordering options and seasonal discounts.

Quality Assurance

All products on our platform are verified for authenticity and quality standards.

Inventory Management

Smart inventory management system to help pharmacies maintain optimal stock levels.

Training & Support

Comprehensive training and 24/7 support for all platform users.

How It Works

For Pharmacies

  1. Register your pharmacy on CareKaro
  2. Browse through verified manufacturers and their products
  3. Place orders directly with manufacturers
  4. Track your orders in real-time
  5. Receive deliveries and manage inventory

Register as Pharmacy

For Manufacturers

  1. Register your manufacturing unit on CareKaro
  2. List your products with detailed information
  3. Receive orders directly from pharmacies
  4. Process orders and arrange for delivery
  5. Manage payments and grow your network
